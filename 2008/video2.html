General-Market Digital: “White Gold”

We learned that milk consumption drops off among teens, so we went after them directly. We created a fictional rock star named White Gold, who, with the help of his dairy-filled guitar, headlined as a spokesman for milk in a way that would appeal to the younger generation and make drinking milk seem “cool.” White Gold’s spandex outfit, moustache, crazy hair and witty milk-themed lyrics were just the ticket for pulling in the teen audience, with YouTube views topping tens of thousands.
